International Tumor Board


At American Oncology Institute, a team of expert oncologists and faculty from top US Hospitals collaborate to
review and discuss complex cancer cases.

Multidisciplinary Approach

The tumor board includes specialists from various disciplines, such as medical oncology, radiation oncology, surgical oncology, pathology, radiology, and other relevant fields. This ensures that all aspects of a patient's care are considered.

International Faculty

The board members comprise of eminent faculty from UPMC, bringing diverse perspectives and expertise to the discussion.

Virtual Collaboration

Meetings of an international tumor board are often conducted virtually, allowing experts from different locations to participate without the need for travel.

Case Presentation and Discussion

Patient's case, including relevant clinical information, imaging studies, pathology results, and treatment history is presented to the board. The board then discusses the case and provides recommendations on the treatment of the patient.

Treatment Recommendations

The international tumor board provides recommendations on the optimal treatment approach for the patient, considering the latest evidence-based guidelines, available treatment options, and the patient's preferences and values.
